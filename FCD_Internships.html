Addressed to All Internship Sponsors

The Computer Science Internship Program at Northeastern Illinois University has provided successful internships at over a hundred Illinois companies, government agencies, and organizations. Many of our former interns still work for the companies or organizations which provided them with internships.

Internships

  • Can start any time of the year.
  • Can be part-time or full-time.
  • Many are paid but we welcome all internship opportunities
  • The CS department actively solicits internships and acts as an interested and active third party
  • Many students research and line up their own internships

We ask for information to assist you

  • We ask for your requirements and a description of the internship project.
  • Use your own format, send a link on your website, or fill in a form. Request for participation
  • We post your requirements on our website and listserv, and post on a bulletin board
  • The sponsor interviews and selects the best candidate
  • We provide interviewing facilities in cooperation with our Career Placement office.

Students can earn academic credit for internships - normally 3 credit hours, sometimes more

  • This involves the student, the sponsor, and the internship coordinator
  • We sign an Internship Agreement describes the project, learning objectives, and grading criteria
  • Students maintain a portfolio of assignments, work papers, log, and learning summary
  • The interns and internship coordinator meet on a regular basis usually every 2-3 weeks
  • The intern's supervisor critiques the intern.
  • A grade is given at the end of the semester
